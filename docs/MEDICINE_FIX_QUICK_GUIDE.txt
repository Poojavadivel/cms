================================================================================
                    QUICK GUIDE: MEDICINE OUT OF STOCK FIX
================================================================================

🎯 WHAT WAS THE PROBLEM?
================================================================================

When doctors tried to save intake forms with medicines, they saw:
❌ "Medicine X is out of stock"

This made doctors think:
- They couldn't prescribe the medicine
- There was a bug in the system
- The form wouldn't save

================================================================================
✅ WHAT'S FIXED NOW?
================================================================================

1. **CLEARER STOCK DISPLAY**
   
   BEFORE in dropdown:
   Paracetamol 500mg - ₹5 (Stock: 0)
   
   AFTER in dropdown:
   Paracetamol 500mg - ₹5 (⚠️ OUT OF STOCK)
   
   → Much more visible!

2. **BETTER WARNING MESSAGES**
   
   BEFORE:
   "Paracetamol is out of stock"
   
   AFTER:
   "⚠️ Paracetamol is currently out of stock (Pharmacist will be notified)"
   
   → Explains what happens next!

3. **FORM STILL SAVES**
   
   - You CAN prescribe out-of-stock medicines
   - The prescription will be saved
   - Pharmacist will handle stock issues
   - This is how it should work!

================================================================================
📋 HOW TO USE IT NOW:
================================================================================

STEP 1: Open Intake Form
- Click on a patient appointment
- Click "Intake" button

STEP 2: Add Medicine
- Go to "Pharmacy" section
- Click "Add Medication"
- Select medicine from dropdown

STEP 3: Check Stock Status
- If you see: "Stock: 10" → Medicine is available ✅
- If you see: "⚠️ OUT OF STOCK" → Medicine needs restocking ⚠️

STEP 4: Prescribe Anyway
- Fill in dosage, frequency, duration
- Click "Save Intake Form"
- ✅ Form saves successfully!

STEP 5: Pharmacist Handles It
- Pharmacist sees the warning
- Pharmacist orders more stock OR
- Pharmacist contacts you for alternative OR
- Pharmacist notifies patient about delay

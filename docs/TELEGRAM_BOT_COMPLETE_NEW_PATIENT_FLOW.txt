----------------------

   - Existing Patient → Quick lookup flow

2. NEW PATIENT REGISTRATION FLOW (Step by Step):
   ------------------------------------------------
   Step 1: Full Name
   Step 2: Phone Number
   Step 3: Blood Group (with buttons: A+, A-, B+, B-, O+, O-, AB+, AB-, Don't Know)
   Step 4: Address (full address in text)
   Step 5: Emergency Contact Name
   Step 6: Emergency Contact Phone Number
   Step 7: Reason for Visit
   Step 8: Date & Time (natural language)
   Step 9: Confirmation (shows all details)
   Step 10: Appointment Created

3. EXISTING PATIENT FLOW (Quick):
   --------------------------------
   Step 1: Phone Number or Patient ID (lookup)
   Step 2: Reason for Visit
   Step 3: Date & Time (natural language)
   Step 4: Confirmation (shows basic details)
   Step 5: Appointment Created

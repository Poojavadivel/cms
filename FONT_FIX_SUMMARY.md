## Why Inter?

1. **Modern & Professional** - Used by top companies (GitHub, Linear, Vercel)
2. **Excellent Readability** - Designed for UI/screens
3. **Consistent Weight Scale** - 400, 500, 600, 700 look great
4. **Clean Lines** - Not too fancy, not too plain
5. **Open Source** - Free and optimized
